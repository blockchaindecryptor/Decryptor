The Whitepaper

This is where we talk about our technical solutions for the aggregation of real world datastreams or oracles. This process allows blockchains to have the ability to interact with the world in real time and greatly increases their versatility.

The next generation of oracles will bring about new industies to the crypto-sphere, allowing users and developers alike to take full control of their applications and the data that they use.
